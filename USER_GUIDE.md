# YiW Field Report Mobile App - User Guide

## Welcome to YiW Field Report

This mobile application is designed for SEG Ghana's Youth in Work (YiW) Programme field officers to efficiently collect and submit daily field reports. The app streamlines the reporting process, ensures data accuracy, and automatically distributes reports to relevant stakeholders.

## Getting Started

### Installation

1. **Download the app** from Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS)
2. **Open the app** and you'll see the splash screen
3. **Login** with your credentials provided by SEG Ghana

### First-Time Setup

When you first open the app:
1. **Allow permissions** for camera, location, and storage
2. **Complete your profile** with your details
3. **Familiarize yourself** with the dashboard

## Main Features

### 1. Dashboard
The dashboard provides an overview of your reporting activity:
- **Total Reports**: Number of reports you've submitted
- **This Month**: Reports submitted this month
- **Youth Placed**: Total youth placed in employment
- **Partners**: Number of partner companies engaged

### 2. Creating a New Report

To create a new field report:

1. **Tap the "+" button** or "New Report" from the menu
2. **Fill out the multi-step form** (8 sections):

#### Step 1: Focal Person Details
- Enter your full name and phone number
- Select your zone/region from the dropdown
- Choose the date of visit
- Select visit type(s) (you can select multiple)

#### Step 2: Training Centre Details
- Select the Hub/Training Service Provider
- Enter community name and centre name
- Add centre address and contact information
- Record arrival and departure times

#### Step 3: Attendance Count
- Use the +/- buttons to count:
  - Young men present
  - Young women present
  - Persons with disability
  - Hub staff on duty
  - Trainers/facilitators present
- The app automatically calculates totals

#### Step 4: Employment & Placement Outcomes
- Record youth activated today
- Count placements in:
  - Formal employment
  - Internships
  - Cooperatives/self-employment
  - Referred for further training
- Enter names of youth placed
- Add employer/cooperative names
- Select sector of placement

#### Step 5: Training & Quality
- Rate overall performance (1-5 scale)
- Select quality indicators observed
- Flag any issues found
- Mark available facilities
- Note activities observed
- Describe challenges and recommendations

#### Step 6: Partner Engagement
- Add partner companies engaged today
- For each company, provide:
  - Company name and location
  - Sector and business profile
  - Skills needed
  - Contact information
  - Status (Prospective/Meeting held/Verified/etc.)
  - Youth slots available
- Track progress toward 15 partners/zone target

#### Step 7: Safeguarding Checklist
- Confirm safeguarding measures upheld:
  - Consent obtained before photos/videos
  - Two-adult rule maintained
  - Safeguarding policy visible
  - No discriminatory language
  - Reporting mechanism communicated
  - ID badge worn
  - Professional boundaries maintained
  - Gifts followed guidelines
- Report any safeguarding concerns

#### Step 8: Review & Submit
- Review all entered information
- Add final notes if needed
- Submit the report

### 3. Saving Drafts

If you need to pause while filling out a report:
1. **Tap "Save Draft"** in the top right corner
2. **Resume later** from the "Saved Drafts" section
3. **Edit and submit** when ready

### 4. Taking Photos & Videos

To capture media:
1. **Tap the camera icon** in the media section
2. **Choose** between:
   - Take Photo: Use device camera
   - Browse Gallery: Select from device storage
3. **Add captions** to describe the media
4. **Videos**: Record training activities or testimonials

### 5. Scanning Documents

To scan documents:
1. **Tap "Scan / Take Photo"** in the document section
2. **Position the document** within the frame
3. **Capture** the image
4. **Review** and retake if needed
5. **Save** the scanned document

### 6. Viewing Reports

To view your submitted reports:
1. **Go to "Reports"** tab in the bottom navigation
2. **Browse** through your report history
3. **Tap a report** to view details
4. **Filter** by date, zone, or status

### 7. Offline Mode

The app works offline:
1. **Fill out reports** without internet connection
2. **Save drafts** locally on your device
3. **Auto-sync** when connection is restored
4. **Manual sync** available in settings

## Report Submission Process

When you submit a report, the following happens automatically:

1. **Data Validation**: The app checks for completeness and accuracy
2. **Local Storage**: Report is saved to your device
3. **Cloud Upload**: Report is sent to Firebase (when online)
4. **Email Distribution**:
   - **CEO** receives full report
   - **Registration Officer** receives report for their zone
   - **You** receive confirmation email
5. **Google Sheets**: Data is added to central database
6. **Media Upload**: Photos and documents are uploaded to cloud storage

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't login?
- Check your email and password
- Use "Forgot Password" to reset
- Contact support if issues persist

#### Camera not working?
- Check camera permissions in device settings
- Restart the app
- Try on a different device

#### Report not submitting?
- Check internet connection
- Save as draft and try later
- Ensure all required fields are filled

#### Data not syncing?
- Check internet connection
- Go to Settings → Sync Data
- Restart the app

#### App crashing?
- Update to latest version
- Clear app cache
- Restart your device

### Error Messages

| Error | Meaning | Solution |
|-------|---------|----------|
| "Network Error" | No internet connection | Connect to Wi-Fi or mobile data |
| "Invalid Email" | Email format incorrect | Check email format |
| "Required Field" | Missing mandatory information | Fill in all required fields |
| "File Too Large" | Media file exceeds size limit | Compress or resize the file |
| "Upload Failed" | File couldn't be uploaded | Check connection and retry |

- **Regular Backups**: Automatic backups to Google Sheets

### Privacy
- Your personal data is protected
- Reports are only shared with authorized personnel
- You can request data deletion
- No data is sold to third parties

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Yes! You can fill out reports offline. They'll sync automatically when you're back online.

**Q: How many photos can I attach?**
A: You can attach up to 10 photos per report.

**Q: Can I edit a submitted report?**
A: You can view submitted reports but cannot edit them. Contact support for corrections.

**Q: What if I lose my phone?**
A: Your data is backed up in the cloud. Login from a new device to access your reports.

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to Settings → Profile → Change Password.

**Q: Can I export my reports?**
A: Yes! Go to Settings → Export Data to download as Excel or PDF.

**Q: Who receives my reports?**
A: Reports are sent to the CEO, Registration Officer for your zone, and saved to the central database.

**Q: What if I make a mistake in a report?**
A: Save as draft, make corrections, then submit. Once submitted, contact support for changes.
